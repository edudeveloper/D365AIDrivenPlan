The Challenge

Reshaping D365FO delivery
for the AI era

The market is changing fast. Our competitors are already building AI-powered delivery Agents for ERP projects. If we do not act now, we lose ground on cost, speed, and how we use our talent. We need to give our teams AI tools that are transparent, tunable, and measurably faster — not as a future experiment, but as a real delivery advantage today.

⚡

Cost-effective at scale

Every hour we spend writing FDDs, TDDs, X++ code, and review reports by hand is an hour our competitors save with AI. We need to deliver more with the same team size — turning senior knowledge into a multiplier, not a bottleneck.

Consultants Developers Tech Leads
🔍

Transparent, not a black box

AI tools that produce results no one can explain will be rejected — by our teams and by our clients. Every decision the agent makes must be traceable to a real source. Trust comes from evidence.

Tech Leads Architects Clients
🎛️

Tunable by the delivery team

No two D365FO projects are the same. Consultants shape FDD templates, developers control naming conventions and code patterns, tech leads define review rules. All of this is done through Git-versioned configuration files — no one needs to change agent code. The team owns the rules; the agents follow them.

Output — Dual Artifacts

TDD + Execution Prompt

The TDD is the human-readable design document. The Execution Prompt is a structured Markdown contract (schema per object) consumed by the X++ Agent — containing AOT type, action (create/extend), base object, method signatures, field specs, extension points, dependencies, and Ninja MCP evidence trail.
